Design Equipix

The RULES

A Bosslogic meets Steve Wozniak Hackathon!

Class: IX-XII

Team Size: 1-4 Students

1 Team Per School

Software Allowed:

Adobe Photoshop
Adobe Illustrator
Adobe InDesign
Adobe XD
Any Code Editor/Compiler

Event Guidelines

This is a Competition to show off your Participants will be given a Prompt and Design Skills.
They have to Design a
1. Branding Project (Logo, Typeface, Colors etc.)
2. Website Development (HTML, CSS and JavaScript)
3. Poster (Size: US Letter, Poster showing off the Main Product of the Company/Brand) and
4. UI Design Project (3-5 Page App UI Design with Shopping Cart Feature)
Use of Pre-Made Templates is Prohibited
Only Google Fonts must be Used(fonts.google.com).
Use Stock Images for Pastries and Cakes.
Prompt will be released on Devfolio and the link for the same will be put on the Whatsapp Group and Discord Server
All Submissions will be entertained on Devfolio only.
Participants of Winning Teams (Top 3) will be home-delivered Schwags from Devfolio.

Event Prompt

Prompt: A Bakery with fusion cakes and pastries, with its Highlighted Product as “Choco Pineapple” Pastry. Its Target audience is Teenagers and Young People liking Fusion Products. The Bakery Requires a complete Redesign of Brand Identity.
Name: FuLicious
Products Offered: Fusion Cakes and Pastries, Donuts, Shakes and On-The-Go Food.
Logo Requirement: A Clean Logo design that will look good on the Company Store Banner and also attracts its Target Audience.
Typography: Something Clean
Brand Colors: Upto the Designer
UI/UX: An online app to view all the products offered. Must have a add to cart feature and Cart Page.
Website: View all products and must have a add to cart feature. Must Showcase the special “Choco Pineapple” Party on the Homepage
Poster: Poster to be put on Instagram and Facebook. Include a Call to Action and showcase photo to the Special “Choco Pineapple Pastry” Include the Website URL and Company Logo.

Judgement Criteria

Originality: 5
Usability: 5
Coding Skills: 5
Design Aesthetics: 5
Features Used: 5
Total: 25

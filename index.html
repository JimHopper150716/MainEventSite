About WARTEX

One of the most highly anticipated school tech event in Delhi. The event regularly attracts a diverse range of attendees from almost all Delhi schools, across different classes; and offers an enthralling experience to last the whole year. But this year, we are going bigger than ever, and because WARTEX is online, this year we are accepting registrations from all around the Globe.

850+ Students

55+ Schools

13 Events

1 ULTIMATE COMPETITION

As now the world strides with the revolution of technology, we are experiencing a perpetual transofmation in our lives. To be steady with our motion of development, it is vital for the youth to grasp every facet of technology and innovation.

S.R.D.A.V. has undertaken an effort to contribute to this field of learning. Technocrats, the computer club of S.R.D.A.V. has managed to host the annual wartex event since 2015. Launching with 12 events and participation from 350+ students from 18+ schools, we have come a long way to 850+ participants from 55+ schools for 18 events in Wartex #5.0 in 2019, with a few celebrities, such as Harsh Beniwal, Manoj Bakshi, Gaurav Arora, Pratishtha Sharma among others, gracing the event with their presence as well.
